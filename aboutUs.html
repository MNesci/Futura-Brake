About Us

At Futura Brake, we provide our customers with premium brake pads for the most popular makes and models on the market. We have provided brakes to the Toronto and GTA area for taxi and limousine fleets for more than 20 years, and have thousands of satisfied customers across Canada. Choose Futura Brakes for established and recognized quality at unbeatable prices.

Commercial Grade Brakes for Taxis and Trucks

At Futura Brake, we specialize in high quality brake pads that have been trusted by the taxi and limousine industry for over 20 years, and have been used in thousands of vehicles across many fleets. Our brake pads have been further put to the test by large work vehicles, such as cargo vans and Hino trucks. Whether you need brakes for taxis, brakes for vans, or brakes for trucks, Futura Brake has a brake fit for your model.
