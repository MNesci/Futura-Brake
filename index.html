High Quality Brake Pads

Our exclusive Futura Brakes are premium brake pads at an affordable price. With over 20 years of experience and thousands of satisfied customers across Canada, you can rest assured that when you buy a Futura Brake, you are buying quality.

Futura Brakes are made in North America with the highest quality control standards. Our semi-metallic brakes are made with positive mold technology for the best possible quality, providing better stopping power, and longer brake pad and rotor life. Our brakes are made with an AASA Leafmark compliant eco-friendly formulation, and create little to no dust or noise. They are made to O.E specifications and include sensors/wires where applicable.
